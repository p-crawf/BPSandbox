1st Commandment: No Other Gods

God gives Israel the Ten Commandments after rescuing them from slavery in Egypt. The first commandment — "you shall have no other gods before me" — is the foundation for everything that follows. But what does it mean to put God first? And why does the order matter? In this video, we explore how the 1st commandment is really an invitation to worship the one true God who acts on our behalf.

Reflect

Discussion Questions

  1. 1.What are some "gods" that compete for our loyalty and trust in today's culture? How do they subtly claim the place that belongs to God?
  2. 2.The first commandment is given after God rescues Israel from Egypt. Why does the redemption story come before the command? What does that order tell us about the nature of God's relationship with his people?
  3. 3.How does placing God first practically shape the way you make decisions, spend your time, and order your relationships?
  4. 4.Jesus summarized the law as loving God and loving neighbor. How does the 1st commandment anchor that summary? What happens when either love is out of order?
