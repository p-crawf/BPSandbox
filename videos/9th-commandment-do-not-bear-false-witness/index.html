9th Commandment: Do Not Bear False Witness

A common summary for the 9th Commandment is "Do not lie." But it actually means "Do not bear false witness against your neighbor," which has a very specific context in mind: a law court. The 9th Commandment is about protecting the vulnerable from those who would use twisted truth to destroy them. But more than that, it's a call to be people of truth in all of public life.
